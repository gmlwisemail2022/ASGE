What is Neobanking?

Virtual or neo banks in Hong Kong are banks operating without any physical branches.

They provide banking services such as deposits, payments, and transfers through secure online and mobile platforms. Launched as part of Hong Kong's initiative to promote fintech innovation, these banks offer a convenient, fast, and user-friendly alternative to conventional banking.

They offer retail services either by securing their license or partnering with other licensed banks, often featuring competitive fees, round-the-clock access, real-time transactions, and innovative financial solutions.


Traditional banking v/s Neo banking

Traditional Banking

  • Operates both physical and online branches
  • Higher, often hidden fees
  • Varying onboarding requirements, which can be time-consuming
  • Minimum balance limit varies per bank

Neo Banking

  • Fully online operations with no physical branches
  • Lower, more transparent fees
  • Fast and efficient account opening
  • No minimum balance requirement
