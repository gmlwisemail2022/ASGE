Company Secretarial Services

Asia SGE offers comprehensive company secretarial services, covering aspects such as company incorporation and registration, annual return submissions, shareholder management, and corporate governance advice.

Leveraging our local expertise, we ensure your entities comply with all legal requirements.

In essence, we simplify the complexities of running your business.

What does this service include?

  1. Smooth Compliance and Regulatory Support:

    Provision of a registered address, preparation and filing of annual renewals and other compliance reminders.

  2. Company Formation and Governance:

    Maintenance of the Significant Controller Register, preparation of written resolutions for AGM, maintenance of minutes of meetings.

  3. Corporate Actions:

    Issuing, allotment, and transfer of shares, appointment and change of company directors.

  4. Issuing relevant documents:

    Certificate of Incumbency, attestation of UBO’s and shareholding structure.
