What does this service include?

Our comprehensive services encompass the structuring and negotiation of ordinary equity, equity-linked, or debt capital raising. Our holistic approach merges strategy, preparation, and execution to ensure a successful capital raise.

Step 1: Evaluating Your Company's Capital Needs: Involves assessing whether the capital raising is for growth initiatives, shareholder liquidity, or acquiring another venture. By analyzing these factors, we can determine the appropriate strategy to ensure tax efficient capital structure.

Step 2: Detailed Valuation Analysis: Conducting an in-depth valuation analysis to estimate your company's valuation, aid in revenue forecasting, and perform competitor research.

Step 3: Crafting and Positioning Your Pitch: Creating your company's unique proposition to a curated group of potential investors or lenders who are the right fit for your venture.

Step 4: Communication and Marketing Documents: Generating teasers, memorandums, and management presentations to effectively communicate your venture's potential.

Step 5: Maintaining and Building Investor Relationships: Proactively maintaining transparency, engagement, and momentum by building interactions with potential investors or lenders.

Step 6: Negotiation with Potential Investors: Negotiating with potential investors regarding structuring, timing, pricing, and financing terms. If required, creating a multilayer structure to help you raise funds from multiple jurisdictions.
