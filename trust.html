What does the process look like?

The Framework for the trust is outlined in the Trust Deed

The trust deed is a legal document between the Settlor (the person putting the assets into the trust) and the Trustee, outlining how the assets will be managed and distributed.

Trust Deed reflects your specific needs

Various clauses can be written in the trust deed detailing your needs. These clauses can be about how the funds are to be distributed to the beneficiaries- whether in equal terms or percentage terms or the life of the Trust can be specified. We ensure your Trust Deed reflects your wishes.

We work closely with you, guiding you through the entire process from selecting the right jurisdiction to establish the trust to aligning the trust deed to reflect your purpose behind creating the trust.

Trustees - Your Asset Protectors

The Trustee assumes legal ownership of the assets and has a professional duty to care for them on behalf of the beneficiaries.
