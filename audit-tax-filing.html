Hong Kong Corporate Tax Filing

In Hong Kong, companies are required to file Profit Tax returns, encompassing the business transactions conducted during the relevant period.

Our service includes:

  • Profit Tax Computation
  • Offshore Tax Claim: If the income is sourced outside Hong Kong
  • Tax Filing with Inland Revenue Department


Individual Tax Advisory & Planning

Individuals report employment income, profits from sole proprietorship businesses, rental income etc to the revenue authorities along with any allowances/deductions claimed during the relevant period.

Our service includes:

  • Remuneration Packages: We aid in structuring remuneration packages in a tax-effective manner.
  • Tax Computation
  • Tax Filing with Inland Revenue Department
